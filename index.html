About Us

Gyumed Tara Temple is dedicated to preserving the rich traditions of Tibetan Buddhism. Our mission is to spread teachings of compassion, wisdom, and peace through various ceremonies, teachings, and community events.

Partner Monastery

Our partner monastery, Gyudmed Tantric Monastery in India, plays a crucial role in preserving and transmitting the teachings of Tibetan Buddhism. The monks are known for their deep practice of tantra and their commitment to world peace through spiritual activities.

Announcement

2024 Gyudmed Tantric Monastery Happiness Sand Mandala Tour

Gyudmed Tara Temple is proud to announce that we will be organizing the ‘2024 Gyudmed Tantric Monastery Happiness Sand Mandala Tour’ in the United States starting November 1, 2024. The tour will be led by highly trained Tibetan monks from Gyudmed Tantric Institute in India, one of the two most famous tantric schools in the Gelugpa tradition of Tibetan Buddhism. In addition to the main focus of creating a sand mandala for world peace, the monks will also have other activities such as chanting/prayer, providing talks on the Dharma, modeling butter sculptures, and a few sessions on mindfulness meditation.

As we prepare for this first-ever tour, Gyudmed Tara Temple, a proud extension of the Gyudmed Tantric Institute, is requesting your generosity by kindly sponsoring one or two events during the aforementioned tour. We look forward to hearing from you and want to thank you for your time and support in our endeavor to spread this joyous event.
